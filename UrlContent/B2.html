Aadhar Foundation Trust Udaipur

Non-profit organization Foundation For Road Safety

Aadhar Foundation Trust is a non- profit making registered non-governmental organization registered under 80G of income tax act 1961. The foundation is working determinately for road safety and societal activities since 2002. It was registered in 2016 and since then 86000 people of different age have been trained for road safety and 47000 road accident victims are helped with treatment, food and providing basic need such as ration, free education and skill training to members of victim family.

In the present age of technology and development the ever increasing calamities due to road accidents have compelled us to start a war against disastrous accidents on road, wreaking lakhs of lives and property year by year. According to a 2018 national report, India alone has been witnessing 1.5 lakh of road fatalities due to road accidents every year. Of which one-third of people lose their lives in these accidents and many remain permanently disabled for most of their lives. Each year, about three to five percent of the country’s GDP is invested in road accidents. Notably, while India has about one percent of the world’s vehicle population, it also accounted for about six percent of the global road traffic incidents. About 70 percent of accidents involved young Indians. It is our aim to spread awareness on road safety among people to create healthy and safer environment while driving, thereby, helping our country reviving the GDP lost due to roads calamities.

And yet unstoppable, pushing hard in every aspect to make lives better. Our journey doesn’t stop on road safety but endeavoring a new fight against cancer: providing for treatment and basic needs to family members of cancer victim. According to World Health Organization (WHO), cancer is the second leading cause of death globally. In 2018, approximately 18 million cases were there globally, of which, 1.5 million were in India alone. In 2018, there were around 0.8 million cancer deaths in India against 9.8 million globally.

It is our effort to educate our society and youth and prepare them to fight against the threats posed by these calamities to human life. And knowledge is most powerful weapon. Knowing alone can have great impact on betterment of health, life and country. Aadhar foundation welcomes and appreciates you to be the part of our mission.
