What is JS@PayPal?

JS @ PayPal - our JavaScript developer conference is happening on May 19, 20, 21. We’re bringing together our developer community to share all things JavaScript!

Our aim is to connect with our community through a series of talks from our global JavaScript speakers, while we are also committed to supporting the tech communities with equality, diversity and inclusion. Topics will include all things Javascript - Node , React, GraphQL, Redux, UX, performance, accessibility, security, interface design, CSS/JS techniques and all that jazz!

The conference will be livestreamed and talks will be shared on our PayPal Youtube channel. Stay tuned for livestream link.
