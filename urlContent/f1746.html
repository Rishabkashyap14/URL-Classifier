New Report: The Future of Agencies

How does your agency need to adapt to be prepared for what’s ahead?

The customer experience revolution is having a huge impact on agency operating models. The Future of Agencies report, published by Econsultancy in partnership with Adobe, explores key areas including data, technology, skills, and culture, and the ways agencies are adapting across each to continue to create and deliver value for their clients and to stay ahead. It is based on more than 20 in-depth interviews with senior agency and SI leaders and a survey of over 500 practitioners.

The report is written specifically for agencies, and also contains valuable information for brand marketers who work with these companies for their marketing activities.

The Future of Agencies research findings were presented in a three-part webinar series — all now available for on-demand viewing.
