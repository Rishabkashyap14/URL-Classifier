Very clean travel trailer - single owner, non smoking. We bought it in 2012 in WA and used it about 6 times per year from 2012-2018 when we moved from WA to AZ. Now the kids are grown and we are looking to downsize. Everything works and is in very good shape. We upgraded most lights to LED, new faucet new 2x6v batteries, Outdoor grill, and misc. other upgrades. Sleeps up to 6-8 (2 bunk beds, King, couch folds into bed, dining table folds into bed). Air conditioning, electric heat, and propane heat. Awning in good shape, no leaks, original tires and brakes.
I'm able to tow trailer easily with a V8 Jeep Cherokee.
