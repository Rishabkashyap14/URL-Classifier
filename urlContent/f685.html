Premier Electrics Bring The Power!

Thank you to local business Premier Electrics who have recently come on board as a club sponsor.

The Bellaghy based electrical contractor are an award-winning business delivering large scale electrical projects throughout Ireland, UK and Europe. Their advertising signage is now in place and will feature prominently throughout Hatrick Park for the upcoming season.

Tony Shivers, Managing Director said, “Premier Electrics recognises the important role that sport plays in our local communities and the value that it can bring for local people. We are proud to support a range of local sports organisations and wish Rainey Old Boys RFC the very best of luck in the season ahead.”

You can find out more about Premier Electrics on their website: premierelectrics.com .
