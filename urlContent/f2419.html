Welcome

Pavillion Bank is committed to the safety and well-being of our customers, staff and community. We are closely monitoring the current situation and are taking precautionary steps to reduce the potential of exposure and spread of COVID-19.

Following local and federal recommendations, we have made the decision to adjust our banking hours. We will close our lobby effective immediately, but lobby services will be available Monday – Friday from 9:00 AM to 4:30 PM by appointment only. The drive-thru will remain open Monday – Friday from 9:00 AM to 4:30 PM and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. Enhanced drive-thru services will be available during this time. We have implemented protocols to ensure our employees practice social distancing and follow enhanced cleaning procedures. If you visit our drive-thru or ATM in person, we recommend you also wash your hands thoroughly afterward or use hand sanitizers.

As always, online and mobile banking services are available 24/7.

We also strongly encourage you to take advantage of our online banking services and mobile app to access your accounts and financial services during this time. You can:

* Make payments on a loan
* Deposit checks using the remote deposit app
* Pay bills using Bill Pay
* Transfer money between accounts
* Reorder checks
* Request a stop payment
* Change your address
* View check images
* View eStatements

If you haven’t enrolled for online banking or downloaded the mobile app, you can do so at any time. It only takes a few minutes.

We are here to help. We understand that customers may find themselves facing financial difficulties as a result of COVID-19. If you need assistance, please contact us at 972-380-0700.
