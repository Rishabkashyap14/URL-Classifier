Solar Benefits

Environmental Impact

The average vehicle emits about 4.7 metric tons of C02 every year (sources: EPA.gov ). A standard 6kW system (6,000 watts) adequately offsets the emissions produced by one fossil fuel vehicle in a year. Additionally, reducing pollutants can prevent unnecessary health care costs.

Reduced Electric Bills

A typical owned system with a standard 6kW system (6,000 watts) on a shingle rooftop and a utility inflation rate of 3% per year would total up to an estimated $62,080 in savings over a 30 year span in California. An average 84% of power needs met by solar panels is assumed along with the average electricity rate per state ($/KWH) ( EIA )

Example: $18,500 6kW shingle system after rebate = $12,600 . Estimated 30 Year Savings = $85,635.75 – 6kW system at $12,600 = $73,035.75 times utility cost reduction of .85 = $62,080.38 in total estimated savings

Government & State Incentives / Tax Credits

Qualified Homeowners may claim a Residential Renewable Energy Tax Credit of 26% towards expenditures for a Solar PV System issued by the government. Additionally, California Solar Initiative ( CSI ) offers cashback rebates and incentives for installing solar on your home or commercial business. Please contact us for more information and details.
