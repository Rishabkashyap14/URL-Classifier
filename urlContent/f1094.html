WHO ARE WE ?

In this fast-moving world where development and innovation is the key to move forward. A bunch of young entrepreneurs in Visakhapatnam sensed the need for newer technology for the growing needs of the world, So we established Kalam Dream Labs on 27 th July 2015 in fond memory of Shri A.P.J Abdul Kalam. Since 2015 our team has been innovating and setting a new trend of technological improvements in India. Our project “Using UAV technologies for fire fighting” was applauded at USCEI 2015 Conference, New Delhi. More innovative projects are in line for delivery in years to come.

Our vision

To see technological solutions help countries achieve all the 17 global goals.

Our mission

Our primary concern is to work on new ideas which help in better living and work toe to toe in converting the ideas into reality.

ABOUT KALAM DREAM LABS

July 27, 2015 in fond memory of the career scientist turned politician, Shri A.P.J Abdul Kalam, Kalam Dream Labs is a platform open to all the dedicated and creative thinkers to transform latest innovations in technology into fruit full products which indirectly helps every living being to lead a peaceful life on this planet.

We aim at applying global technologies to meet local needs. We believe that if creative minds are converged onto a single focal point, we could achieve much more than the miracles of science and technology.
