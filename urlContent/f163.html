What distinguishes us as Zürcher Kantonalbank?

100% Zürich DNA

As an independent institution under public law of the Canton of Zurich, our thoughts and actions are primarily focused on the Canton of Zurich. We know the economic region of Zurich very well and our customers benefit from this. We think about the future and operate sustainably and socially. Though firmly anchored in Zurich, we also operate nationally and internationally.

Close to you

Our proximity works 360 degrees: we are committed to solving your individual financial needs. We accompany you around the clock with uncomplicated digital channels and welcome you in the densest branch network in the canton of Zurich. What can we do for you?
